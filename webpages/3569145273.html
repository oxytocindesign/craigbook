Test a Game, No Exp Needed (Spokane)

Do you like reading about new Kickstarter projects or current sports and think you're good at predicting outcomes? If so, we need your help.

We are B3TZ (say 'betz'), a new social game currently in Alpha development. And that's where you come in. We need more game testers so we can understand how people want to play and what features, functionality and fun are working (or not!) in our game. In return for testing, you earn cash cards.

Doing this gig is simple. Just go to one of these sites:

>www.kickstarterstandard.com
>www.b3tzsports.com

On each of these blogs are posts about a topic (sports event, Kickstarter project). Read the post and make a choice of AGREE or DISAGREE. That's your position on that b3tz.

For each b3tz you win, you earn b3tz points, our credits. Those credits can then be traded in for cash cards on our Prize Page. The more you test the game the more you can earn. Some of our current testers have earned over $75 over a week or so for minutes a day of testing.

We are also looking for Alpha Testers. Alpha testers play the game every day AND provide feedback in our forum at www.b3tz.com. Become an expert on how to make B3TZ great, contribute feedback, ideas and bug issues and become a B3TZ Expert.

There's nothing to sign up for. If you're interested, just go play. And if you have any questions just shoot us a note. Please, no resumes or job requests.

Thanks for considering this gig.
