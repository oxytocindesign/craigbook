Systems Analyst (Chicago, IL)

Excellent contract opportunity with one of our top clients, a prestigious financial services company in Chicago, with a need for a Systems Analyst to join their team. The length of time for the contract role is expected to be 12 months. As a Systems Analyst, you will analyze business requirements; research and evaluate alternative solutions for new and existing systems; contribute estimates to the project planning effort; work with the development team, operations and technical support throughout the development and installation process; work with the development team to create detailed design of processes; and participate in the writing and reviewing of unit test plans. In addition, you will complete source code analysis as required; develop project lifecycle documents that will describe business and systems requirements; as well as assess testing scope and interface with Q/A to develop detailed system testing plans; and support integration test execution and verification of results. Great, supportive work environment for contractors!

Requirements:
* 3+ years of experience in systems analysis and application program development with strong analytical and problem solving skills. Must be competent to work at all phases of systems development.
* Strong relational database and SQL - ability to write SQL from scratch and experience with unions, joins, case statements, etc.
* Ability to work across multiple platforms ( IBM Mainframe, UNIX, NT, distributed systems ).
* Experience with Microsoft Office desktop tools ( Word, Excel ). Exposure to modeling tools ( e.g. Rational Rose, System Architect, Visio ).
* In-depth knowledge of the testing process is also required.
* Object oriented analysis and design techniques preferred.
* Financial industry ( knowledge of derivatives, futures, options, etc. ) experience is important.
* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Math, Business, Communications or a related discipline, or an equivalent combination of education and work experience.
