Executive Assistant/Leasing Manager (Olney, Germantown, West Mt. Airy)

Are you looking for an exciting career in real estate? Do you have strong administrative skills? Are you detail-oriented and customer service friendly? Do you perform well in a fast-paced environment? If so, this may be the right position for you...

Executive Assistant to Regional Manager:
As the executive assistant, expect your day-to-day responsibilities to be a plethora of non-routine tasks. This is where your exceptional time allocation skills will be needed. Those tasks are not limited to, but include the following:

- Administrative functions
- Answering phones
- Serving tenant needs
- Troubleshooting on-site issues
- Interacting with contractors/vendors

Also, a portion of the time spent at your desk will be heavily paperwork driven by managing the office's operations and the regional manager's calendar. Another component to this position will be maintaining the leasing reports accurately and delivering them timely as requested by the Regional Manager.

Leasing & Staff Management:
One of the most important duties of the leasing manager is the ability to oversee a staff of property managers/leasing agents and on-site property staff. This can be a full-time job in itself as you'll be expected to focus on the overall customer service experience.

Among some of the other duties, you will be required to:

- Ensure that new residents receive what they were promised
- Execute and log repair service orders in order to avoid reoccurring issues
- Make sure the property is well kept

In some cases, you may be expected to work off-site to oversee another property in the event that there is no manager on duty. Lease needs, such as occupancy and lease renewals, must be handled. You must also work to set and maintain a budget and be prepared to report expenses to the Regional Manager.

This is a great opportunity to break into an industry that is both rewarding and challenging.

Send your resume to greg@resi-life.com.
